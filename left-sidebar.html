About Us

Who are we?

Sonia

Hello! I am Sonia and I am a 15-year-old student from Athens, Greece. I have many interests, like triathlon and artistic photography, and I recently took part in an amazing programming experience, "Code Like A Girl", offered by Vodafone. There, I gained many coding skills I wouldn't have been able to imagine before, and so used those skills to make a difference in the world! Given all the unpleasant events happening around us, like the global pandemic and the degradation of the natural environment, I would really like to give people hope, in times when they need it the most! Thus, my teammate and I created this page, to add a spark of hope in people's heart, providing them with positive news that they have most probably never heard about before. I believe that my goal will have been fulfilled if at least one person leaves this webpage feeling more comfortable and optimistic about the world! I hope you enjoy our creation! :)

Eva

I hope you like it!
